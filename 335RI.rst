The OpenIMU335RI Robust Industrial CAN module integrates highly-reliable MEMS inertial
sensors (acceleration, angular rate/gyro, and magnetic field) in a miniature
factory-calibrated package to provide consistent performance through the extreme
operating environments.

OpenIMU335RI has excellent acceleration and gyro performance that matches
systems which are more expensive.
